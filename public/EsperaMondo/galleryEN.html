EsperaMondo Gallery

Map of EsperaMondo

Main Spawn

This is where players begin, and it's known for its big towers and large buildings. This is a safe area for any new players that may need some time to gather resources before moving out.

The sand village

This village is on a small island and it is full of trading posts and villagers. Bring some emeralds here and hope to make a good deal! Or you could just enjoy the beach, of course.

Skandio

Even in the colder parts of the server there's still sights to see. Skandio has taken a while to build because of the harsh northen climate, but despite all odds, it is still a thriving town.

The Far North

As we approach the further parts of the world, there is a relatively unexplored articic area that's characterized by large ice spikes and rather rough terrain.

The Far South

Like the sand village, the temperate climate and appealing landscape has made the south known for its ports and trading. Many players choose to live here and put together resources to continue building up this part of the world.
