Rules of EsperaMondo

  1. Respect other players

    Treat other players with respect and a sence of community and politeness. No kind of politcal or religious discussion are permitted. Our community consists of many different poeple, relgious groups, and poltical groups. For this reason, these kinds of discussions should be refrained from.

  2. Do not steal and do not break

    Do not destroy buildings that other players have created. Do not take anything from other players' chests if they do not give you permission.

  3. Maintain friendly and appropriate behaviour

    EsperaMondo is a server for everybody. Extensive insulting and innapropriate discussions are not permitted in this server.

  4. Use of Esperanto is reccomended

    While it is not against the rules to speak other languages in chat, it is reccomended that you speak in Esperanto to remain inclusive and understandable to other players in the server.
