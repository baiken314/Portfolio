Recent changes

Finally, Esperamodno now uses the Minecraft version 1.11.2! Here are a few features from this version:

  • With firework rockets, you can fly much longer with the elytra.
  • You can now smelt iron tools in a furnace to recieve iron nuggets.
  • There is a new spell that is called "Sweeping Edge," which makes sword attacks stronger.
  • /tempo – shows the time (UTC).
  • /ensaluto – look at the last login from a user.
  • /unua-ensaluto – look at the last logout from a user.

Yo can now make private chests [privata], that are accesible for 2 to 3 other players! Simply type the names of the other players under your name.

On signs, words typed with the x-system now automatically become hatted letters instead, just like in the messager.

There are five new commands:

  • /sudo – takes you to the southern lands, just like the northern lands. So there's a new section of the server.
  • /elitroj – Takes you to the elytra store where you can buy a pair for 16 diamonds.
